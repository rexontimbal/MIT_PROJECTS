Related Agencies

Road safety is a collaborative effort. The following agencies work alongside PNP Caraga on accident prevention and response:

DPWH Region 13

Department of Public Works and Highways — Road infrastructure, safety improvements, and engineering countermeasures for identified hotspots.

LTO Region 13

Land Transportation Office — Driver licensing, vehicle registration, and enforcement of motor vehicle regulations.

LTFRB Region 13

Land Transportation Franchising and Regulatory Board — Public utility vehicle regulation, route management, and passenger safety.

DOH Region 13

Department of Health — Emergency medical response coordination, hospital referrals, and injury surveillance data.
